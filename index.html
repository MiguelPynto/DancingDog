The objective of the game is to block all crabs by pushing bowls. Crabs can crawl in diagonal and are always hunting for you if there's a path. If they pinch you, you lose a life.

There is also a cooperative mode, where you and a friend fight strategically against the annoying crabs.

Extra, scared cats give you lives if you catch them, and only the dog has power to push rocks.

Have phun :)
